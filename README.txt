Transform Game- BUas CMGT Programming Portfolio Game

A puzzle-platformer where you switch between two forms to solve levels.

-Blue & slim: can walk on glass bridges, however cannot push heavy blocks
-Red & wide(-er): cannot walk on glass bridges, but can push heavy blocks
-Reach the goal (yellow tile) to win. Collect stars (optional just a collectible)

Controls:

Move: Arrow keys / WASD 
Switch form: Tab
Restart level: R
Quit: Escape
